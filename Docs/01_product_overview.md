\# Product Overview

\#\# What iRun Is  
iRun is a runner-focused media, commerce, and community platform that also acts as  
an internal operating system for the iRun team.

\#\# Platform Pillars  
1\. Media — trusted running journalism and guides  
2\. Training — practical tools runners use weekly  
3\. Community — clubs, events, challenges  
4\. Commerce — iRun-made-to-order merchandise  
5\. Operations — editorial \+ ecommerce \+ support

\#\# Primary User Types  
\- Readers (anonymous)  
\- Runners (logged-in)  
\- Customers  
\- Members (future)  
\- Contributors  
\- iRun Staff (editorial, support, ops)  
\- Partners (events, sponsors)

\#\# Success Definition  
\- Runners return weekly (not monthly)  
\- Training tools are used more than articles  
\- Support issues are resolved in minutes, not days  
\- Internal workflows replace spreadsheets \+ inboxes
