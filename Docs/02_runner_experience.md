\# Runner Experience (Core Value Layer)

\#\# My iRun Dashboard  
Personalized runner home:  
\- Today’s workout  
\- This week’s mileage  
\- Upcoming events  
\- Weather snapshot  
\- Saved articles & routes  
\- Shoe rotation alerts

\#\# Runner Profile  
\- Goal distance (5K → Ultra)  
\- Current weekly kilometres  
\- Preferred run days  
\- Pace zones  
\- Injury flags (optional/private)  
\- Surface preference (road/trail/mixed)

\#\# Personalisation Engine (Rule-Based)  
Used for:  
\- Content recommendations  
\- Training plan suggestions  
\- Event discovery  
\- Gear suggestions

No black-box ML in early phases.
